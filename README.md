# Sample insight :

* Women are more likely to buy compared to men.
* Maharashtra, Karnatak, UP are the top 3 states.
* Adult age group (30 - 49 ) is max contributing.
* sales high in Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels .

  # conclusion:
  target women customers of age group (30-49) live in Maharashtra, Karnatak,UP by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra .
